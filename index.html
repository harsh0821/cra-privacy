Privacy Policy — Competitor Research Agent

Effective date: October 5, 2025 · Built by Harsh Srivastava

AI Research Agent

Plain-language summary

This Competitor Research Agent helps product designers find UI/UX inspiration by searching public design sites and (optionally) your own Dribbble uploads. It only stores minimal activity logs for debugging and does not share your private files or your Dribbble token. Do not upload sensitive personal data.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Competitor Research Agent (the “Agent”), created and maintained by Harsh Srivastava (“we”, “us”), collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you interact with the Agent. The Agent fetches UI/UX inspiration from public sources (e.g., Dribbble via API, web browsing, and other public design websites).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

— Queries and messages (search terms, prompts).
— Optional uploads (Figma JSON, screenshots) you explicitly send for analysis. Do not upload sensitive personal data.

2.2 Automatically collected info

— Technical metadata: timestamps, request IDs, error logs, IP addresses, browser strings for diagnostics.
— Interaction logs: conversation snippets and results retained briefly for debugging/improvement.

2.3 Third-party data: If you authorize third-party services (e.g., Dribbble), the Agent may access data those services expose to the Agent according to the permissions you grant (for example, your Dribbble shots when you authorize user/shots).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process your requests and return search results and insights.
  • Call third-party APIs (e.g., Dribbble) when you authorize them.
  • Debug errors, monitor health, and improve the Agent’s responses.
  • Comply with legal requests and protect rights, property, or safety.

4. Authentication Tokens & Third-Party Services

When you authorize third-party services (e.g., Dribbble OAuth), access tokens enable API calls. We do not store access tokens permanently unless you explicitly choose to save them for convenience. If stored, tokens are encrypted and can be revoked by contacting harsh@yourdomain.com. The Agent uses third-party services that have their own privacy policies; please review those as well.

5. Data Retention

Conversation logs and metadata are retained for a limited period (typically up to 90 days) for debugging and quality improvement. Uploaded files are retained only as long as needed to complete analysis unless you request otherwise. You may request deletion of your data (see Contact).

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data (including encryption for stored tokens). No system is entirely risk-free — contact us immediately if you suspect misuse.

7. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We may share aggregated, anonymized usage statistics. We may disclose data to comply with laws or to protect rights and safety.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of personal data. To exercise these rights, contact: harsh@yourdomain.com. If you authorized Dribbble, you can also revoke access in your Dribbble account.

9. Children

The Agent is not intended for children under 13 (or older minimum ages under applicable law). We do not knowingly collect information from children below the applicable minimum age.

10. International Transfers

Data may be processed on servers in other countries. We take steps to ensure appropriate protections where required by law.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy occasionally. We will post the updated policy with a new effective date and, where required, notify users of material changes.

12. Contact

Questions, deletion requests, or concerns: harsh@yourdomain.com

GDPR Addendum

If you are an EU resident, you have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data; to restrict processing; and to object to processing. To exercise these rights, contact harsh@yourdomain.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

CCPA Addendum (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request disclosure of the categories of personal data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of sale (we do not sell personal data). To submit a verifiable consumer request, email harsh@yourdomain.com.
